Development Lounges

On Saturday September 20th 2014, the ADI hosted three specialists and international pioneers: Dr. Rana Dajani, artist Sundus Abdel Hadi and Professor Masud Taj who helped us explore the rich relationship between Mind, Space and Society. This theme was the focal point of the lectures and discussions.

The event also included INK TANK, a gallery curated by the Medium and ADI featuring prominent Arab artists.

Envision Arabia Summit 2012

Welcome to the 2nd annual Envision Arabia Summit, better known as EAS12, held in New York City's NYU Kimmel Center from October 6-8th.

After our highly successful summit in 2011, we took your feedback very seriously and are pleased to present you with the new format for this year. We will provide a stimulating venue, inspiring speakers and engaging mentors in order to facilitate the ideas that you will come up with and will develop into full-fledged projects.

Envision Arabia Summit 2011

From October 7th-9th the Arab Development Initiative (ADI) gathered hundreds of young Arabs and non-Arabs alike in Montreal for the sole purpose of developing the Arab world. The result? Action plans and fresh projects that aim to spark innovation and development in the region!
